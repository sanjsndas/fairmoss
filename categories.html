Terms of Privacy

How we tend to your data with the same care we give to our gardens — transparent, respectful, and always in season.

01. Information we collect

Fairmoss Thorn Pavilion collects only the data necessary to cultivate a meaningful experience. This may include:

  • Email address (for newsletters and occasional field notes)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent — anonymized)
  • Optional preferences (favourite plant families, gardening zones)

We never sell or trade your information. It remains rooted in our care, just like a prized perennial.

🌿 Data minimisation is our practice.

02. How we use it

Your data helps us improve content, send relevant guides, and understand what our community values most. We use it for:

  • Sending seasonal gardening tips and updates
  • Improving the Pavilion’s layout and features
  • Occasional surveys to better serve growers

You can unsubscribe at any time — no hard feelings, only gratitude.

03. Your rights & choices

We believe in digital sovereignty. You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any moment

For any request, reach out via the contact page — we respond within 7 days.

04. Data security & third parties

We use industry-standard encryption and store data on secure, EU-based servers. We never share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Our only partners are trusted service providers (e.g., email delivery) who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Like a well-built compost heap, our system is monitored regularly to ensure it stays healthy and secure.

🔒 SSL encrypted · reviewed quarterly

05. Updates to this policy

We review this policy every spring, in line with the growing season. Any changes will be clearly communicated via our newsletter and on this page. Last updated: April 2026.

If you have any questions, please write to us at privacy@fairmossthorn.com — we’d love to hear from you.
