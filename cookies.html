Privacy & the Pavilion

How we tend to your data with the same care we give to the garden.

Our rooted approach

At Fairmoss Thorn Pavilion, we believe that privacy is not a policy — it is a promise. We handle your information with the same patience and attention we give to heirloom seeds. This notice explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

🌱 In short: We collect only what is needed to share stories, improve our garden, and send you the occasional note. We never sell or rent your data.

Information we gather

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with the Pavilion:

  • Correspondence — your name, email address, and message when you contact us via the contact form or newsletter.
  • Usage — basic analytics (pages visited, time spent) to understand what resonates with our community.
  • Preferences — if you subscribe, we note your interests to tailor our seasonal notes.

We do not collect sensitive data, and we never ask for more than what is needed for a gracious conversation.

How we use your information

  • To reply to your messages and inquiries.
  • To send you garden notes, if you have subscribed.
  • To improve the Pavilion experience — we listen to what you read and enjoy.
  • To protect the garden against misuse or spam.

We will never use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

Sharing & third parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted partners who help us operate the Pavilion (such as email delivery services), but only under strict confidentiality agreements. These partners are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

We may also disclose information if required by law, but only when we are satisfied that the request is lawful and proper.

Your choices & rights

You are always in control of your data. You can:

  • Unsubscribe from our letters at any time — every email includes a link to withdraw.
  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete your information.

To exercise any of these rights, simply send word to the Pavilion and we will respond within two sunrises.

Data security & retention

We store your information securely and limit access to those who need it to tend to the Pavilion. We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined here, or as required by law. If you have not interacted with us for two years, we will quietly let your data return to the soil — unless you ask us to keep it.

Cookies & small seeds

We use minimal cookies to remember your preferences and to understand how the garden is visited. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. For a deeper look, see our Cookies Policy.

Changes to this notice

We may update this privacy notice occasionally to reflect changes in practice or regulation. When we do, we will update the date below and invite you to review it. Your continued presence in the Pavilion tells us you are comfortable with the current notice.
